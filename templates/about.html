About Our Project

Understanding and visualizing the threats to education systems across the globe (2020–2025).

Project Overview

The Education Under Attack (2020–2025) project documents threats, challenges, and impacts faced by education systems worldwide due to conflicts, attacks on schools, and policy gaps. This initiative highlights data-driven insights to create awareness and drive global solutions.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To analyze and visualize patterns of attacks on education, and provide actionable insights to policymakers and stakeholders.

Our Vision

A world where every child has safe access to schools without fear of conflict, violence, or discrimination.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge

Lack of reliable real-time data on school attacks made tracking difficult.

Solution

Integrated multiple datasets & NGO reports for higher accuracy.

Challenge

Limited awareness of gender-specific impacts of attacks.

Solution

Focused analysis on disparities and published visual gender-based insights.

Challenge

Policy gaps in high-risk regions left schools vulnerable.

Solution

Provided data-backed recommendations to strengthen education policies.

Timeline (2020–2025)

2020

Initial reports highlight increased targeting of schools in conflict regions.

2021–2022

Data collection expanded to cover multiple continents with case studies.

2023

Integration of visualization dashboards and interactive reports.

2024–2025

Advanced predictive modeling to identify potential high-risk areas for the future.
